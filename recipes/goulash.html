Classic Goulash

Description

This meaty goulash recipes is easy to make on the stovetop but can also be done in a slow cooker.

This easy goulash recipe is quick, easy and wonderfully satisfying

  • Large chopped yellow onion and olive oil
  • Two pounds of lean ground beef
  • Two cans of diced tomatoes and two cans of tomato sauce
  • Fresh garlic, soy sauce, dried Italian herb seasoning, bay leaves
  • Two cups of elbow macaroni
