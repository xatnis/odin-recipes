Homemade Spaghetti Sauce with Ground Beef

Description

This Spaghetti Sauce with Ground Beef is a comforting, classic recipe that brings rich and savory flavors to the dinner table.

It's convenient for storage and meal prep. The sauce can be refrigerated for 3-4 days or frozen up to 6 months. It delivers a satisfying, family-friendly meal that's easy to make and always delicious.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1 medium onion
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 small green bell pepper
  • 1 can diced tomatoes
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried oregano
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • half teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients
  2. Combine ground beef, onion, garlic and green pepper in a large saucepan over medium-high heat. Cook and stir until meat is browned and crumbly and vegetabes are tender, 5 to 7 minutes.
  3. Stir diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste in the pan. Season with oregano, basil salt and pepper. Simmer spaghetti sauce for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
  4. Serve hot and enjoy!
