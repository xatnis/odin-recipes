Pizza Recipe

Description

This home made pizza dough recipe is perfect for when you want a quick and a delicious pizza without the long wait for rising. It's beginner friendly and the total time is just 45 minutes, including prep and cook.

The recipe emphasizes simplicity and accessibility with just a handful of common ingredients and straightforward steps. It's a reliable and a fast way to make homemade pizza.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of warm water
  • 1 package active dry yeast
  • 1 teaspoon white sugar
  • 2 and a half cups of bread flour
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Roll out pizza dough
  2. Shape the pizza dough
  3. Gather all ingredients and preheat the oven to 230 degrees C
  4. Place warm water in bowl and add yeast and sugar. Mix it for about 10 minutes until it starts to get creamy
  5. Add flour, oil and salt to the mixture. Beat it until it's smooth
  6. Let it rest for 5 minutes
  7. Turn dough out onto a lightly floured surface and pat or roll into a 12-inch circle
  8. Transfer to the pizza pan
  9. Spread crust with sauce and toppings of your choice
  10. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown, 15 to 20 minutes.
